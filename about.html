About SNJB (Jain Gurukul)

Nestled in the serene town of Neminagar, Chandwad... SNJB stands as a beacon of knowledge. What began in 1928 as a modest endeavor with just four students has blossomed into a distinguished educational institution nurturing 14,200+ students today.

With a 96-year illustrious journey, we offer a spectrum of education from Engineering to Arts, deeply rooted in eternal human values.
